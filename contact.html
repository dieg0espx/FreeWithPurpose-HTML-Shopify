Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does custom jewelry take?

Custom pieces typically take 4-6 weeks from design approval to completion. This includes consultation, design refinement, handcrafting, and quality inspection. Rush orders may be available—please inquire.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide with tracked, insured shipping. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days. Visit our Shipping Policy for details.

What materials do you work with?

We exclusively use recycled precious metals (gold, silver, brass), ethically-sourced gemstones, and sustainable materials. All pieces are handcrafted by artisan partners who practice fair-trade principles.

Can I visit your studio or workshop?

Our artisans work in studios across Bali, Guatemala, and Mexico. While we don't have a public showroom, we occasionally host virtual studio tours and artisan meet-and-greets. Join our mailing list for announcements.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Each piece comes with specific care instructions. Generally, store in a dry place, avoid harsh chemicals, and cleanse energetically during moon phases. Read our Care & Ritual Guide for detailed practices.
