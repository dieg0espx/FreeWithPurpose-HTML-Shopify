Symbolism About This Piece of Jewelry

Vishuddha

Throat Chakra: Purification & Truth

The center of expression, resonance, and integrity. It reminds us to speak what the soul feels. To express truth through voice, art, and creation. When open and balanced, the voice becomes magnetic, clear, and free.

Velvet

Soft Power & Divine Feminine

More than luxurious fabric, velvet carries sacred resonance, softness with strength, sensuality with sovereignty. It invites us into the body, grounding us in presence. Velvet whispers: "Power doesn't need to shout; it emanates."

The Sacred Womb Portal

Creation & Harmony

The union of the velvet and Vishuddha symbol represents the portal to the Womb of Creation. A bridge between voice, heart, and mind. A remembrance of the womb as sacred space, as the source of creation itself.

Snake

Transformation & Awakening

An ancient emblem of rebirth and sacred feminine energy. It embodies release, renewal, and the rising of inner fire. The awakening of our creative life force and sensual power.

Open Wings

Freedom & Ascension

Across cultures, wings symbolize transformation, protection, and divine guardianship, from Isis to the angels. They remind us that we can be both human and divine, grounded and soaring.

Sun

Illumination & Life Force

A radiant reminder of the light within and around us, the eternal rhythm of warmth, creation, and divine order. The heart of the cosmos, and of every sunrise and sunset that moves us.

Yin & Yang

Harmony & Wholeness

The eternal dance of complementary forces. Light and shadow, movement and stillness, creating balance, flow, and unity.

Bronze

Ancient Strength & Alchemy

Forged from recycled materials, bronze unites copper and tin, the masculine and feminine, into enduring beauty. Used in ancient altars and sacred tools, it embodies resilience, grounding, and wisdom.
