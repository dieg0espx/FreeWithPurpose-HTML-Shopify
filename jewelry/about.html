About Mayte B

A Journey of Beauty, Consciousness, and Remembrance

"Jewelry, for me, is not decoration, it's devotion. It's the way I translate ancient wisdom into something that can be worn close to the heart."

Mayte Bluestein is the creator and soul behind Mayte B Jewelry, one of the three portals of Free With Purpose. Her work is the result of a lifelong journey that intertwines spirituality, art, and a deep reverence for nature.

Through her jewelry, Mayte brings together the sacred and the sustainable, creating adornments that honor the Earth, celebrate ancient wisdom, and inspire meaningful connection.

Roots & Inspiration

Mayte's inspiration flows from the meeting point of cultures, symbols, and sacred traditions. Having always been drawn to art, ritual, and the mysteries of creation, she sees jewelry as a way to carry energy, a bridge between the physical and the divine.

Each piece is born from her fascination with ancient civilizations like Egypt and their deep understanding of beauty as a spiritual act. She draws from sacred symbology, the Sun, the Snake, Yin & Yang, and the Chakras, using them as living languages that speak to the subconscious and awaken remembrance.

"Symbols are the language of the soul, they bypass logic and speak directly to the heart."

Spiritual Background

Mayte's creative process is guided by ritual and meditation. Her pieces are infused with intention, designed to align energy and emotion. She believes that adorning oneself is a sacred act, a moment to reconnect with one's essence and with Mother Earth.

This spiritual sensitivity is present in every detail: from the ethical sourcing of materials to the mindful collaboration with artisans who share her respect for tradition and craft.

"There is no true luxury without consciousness. Beauty without awareness is incomplete."

A Vision of Conscious Luxury

Mayte's vision extends beyond jewelry. Through her larger platform, Free With Purpose, she seeks to redefine luxury as something purposeful, an expression of integrity, sustainability, and artistry that uplifts both the maker and the wearer.

Her dream is to create spaces, physical and digital, where beauty, ritual, and community coexist. A place where mothers, daughters, friends, and generations can gather to choose pieces that mark their journeys and celebrate life's transitions with intention.

Empowerment Through Remembrance

Unlike conventional notions of empowerment, Mayte believes we are already powerful, what we need is remembrance. Her work is a call to return to authenticity, to honor the sacred feminine and masculine within, and to live in harmony with nature.

Every jewel is a whisper of that remembrance, a companion on the path of awareness.

"Through sacred design and mindful creation, I seek to remind us all that freedom and purpose are not opposites, they are one."

Sustainability & Ethics

Every piece tells a story, of rebirth, harmony, and remembrance, a story that continues the moment you choose to wear it.
