Ritual & Care

Each Mayte B piece is born from sacred intention and deserves to be treated as a ritual object, one that holds energy, memory, and love. Caring for your jewelry is an act of gratitude and awareness. These simple rituals help you honor its spirit and keep its energy alive.

Step One

Setting Your Intention

Before wearing your piece for the first time, take a quiet moment. Hold it in your hands, close your eyes, and breathe deeply. Whisper an intention, affirmation, or prayer into it, something you wish to remember, embody, or create. The jewelry becomes your ally in carrying that energy throughout your day.

Step Two

Cleansing the Energy

Like us, jewelry absorbs energy from its surroundings. Cleanse it periodically using one of these gentle methods:

  • Place it under the light of the full moon overnight.
  • Leave it on a piece of selenite, quartz, or amethyst.
  • Pass it through incense smoke or palo santo with gratitude.

Avoid harsh chemicals or ultrasonic cleaners, they disturb both material and spirit.

Step Three

Physical Care

All pieces are handcrafted from sustainable metals such as silver, bronze, and gold vermeil.

  • Store them in a soft cloth or pouch to prevent scratches.
  • Keep away from perfumes, lotions, and prolonged moisture.
  • Gently polish with a natural jewelry cloth to restore shine.

Treat each piece as a treasure meant to last for generations, timeless, intentional, alive.

Step Four

Daily Ritual

Wearing your jewelry can become part of your daily ritual. As you put it on, take a breath and reconnect with your intention. Let it remind you of your light, your power, your connection to the sacred.

Over time, it becomes more than adornment, it becomes a companion in your spiritual journey.

May every piece you wear remind you of beauty, purpose, and harmony with the Earth. When cared for with love, these jewels become living symbols of consciousness, heirlooms of the soul.

"There is no compromise between quality and conscience, only beauty with meaning."
