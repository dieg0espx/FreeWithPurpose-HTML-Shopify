Nashami Beadwork

Handcrafted empowerment. Modern African design.

Nashami Beadwork represents a new generation of Samburu women: confident, creative, and economically independent.

In Isiolo, young women have come together to carry their ancient beading tradition into the modern world while courageously breaking away from outdated practices such as polygamy and early marriage.

Each piece tells a story of strength, freedom, and cultural pride.

Their elegant, contemporary designs set new standards: refined, precise, sophisticated—and unmistakably Samburu.

What began as a jewelry collection has grown into a brand for authentic African interior décor. From intricate wall art to modern signature pieces, the Nashami artisans create unique works that reinterpret tradition and give every space a distinctive character.

Curated in Collaboration With

Africa Untamed

Africa Untamed is a regenerative travel company based in Nairobi, dedicated to connecting conscious travelers with meaningful, landscape-anchored experiences across Africa.

They craft bespoke safaris that immerse guests in the continent's extraordinary ecological and cultural diversity, while directly supporting the places and communities they visit.

Their mission is to champion biodiversity conservation and sustainable community development through travel. They partner with grassroots initiatives, conservancies, and community-led projects to ensure that every journey contributes to healthier ecosystems, strengthened livelihoods, and long-term resilience for both people and wildlife.
