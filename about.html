Our Story

The Journey

Born from decades of seeking ancestral wisdom across 60+ countries, Free with Purpose is a sacred portal connecting Earth's treasures with conscious souls.

Our Beginning

A Movement for Conscious Living

Free with Purpose began as a whisper—a call to honor Mother Earth, celebrate artisanal traditions, and create jewelry infused with meaning. What started as a personal journey of healing and transformation has blossomed into a global movement.

Every piece we create carries the essence of ancient wisdom, sustainable practices, and sacred symbolism. We believe jewelry should be more than decoration—it should be a talisman, a companion, a reflection of your authentic self.

Our Philosophy

Earth-Honoring Excellence

We source recycled metals, ethical gemstones, and work with artisans who preserve ancestral techniques. Each creation honors the planet and the hands that craft it, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices throughout our supply chain.

From the mines to your hands, we trace every step, ensuring our jewelry carries not just beauty, but integrity. This is conscious luxury—where style meets soul, and adornment becomes activism.

Our Journey

Milestones & Magic

Every chapter has shaped who we are and deepened our commitment to Earth and soul.

1990s
Seeds of Wanderlust

Mayte's journey begins—traveling through 60+ countries, learning from indigenous wisdom keepers, artisans, and healers. Each experience plants seeds for what would become Free with Purpose.

2010
Mayte B Jewelry is Born

The first collection launches, blending sacred symbols with ethical materials. Jewelry as ritual, as intention, as art—a new paradigm emerges.

2015
Portal to Artisans Opens

Partnerships with global makers deepen. Fair trade, cultural preservation, and artisan empowerment become core pillars of the brand.

2018
Sustainability Commitment

Full transition to recycled metals and ethically sourced gemstones. The Portal to Sustainability launches, curating conscious brands across beauty, fashion, and home.

2023
Free with Purpose Movement

The three portals unite under one vision—Mayte B Jewelry, Sustainability & Well-Being, and Artists & Artisans Market. A global community of conscious seekers gathers.

Today
Expanding the Circle

Building partnerships, launching new collections, and deepening our impact. The journey continues—and we invite you to walk with us.
